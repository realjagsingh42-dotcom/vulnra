Pricing FAQ

Do I need a credit card to start?
No. The Free tier requires only an email address. You only need a payment method when upgrading to Pro.
Can I pay in Indian Rupees (INR)?
Yes. Indian users can pay in INR via Razorpay. International users are billed in USD via Lemon Squeezy. Both options appear at checkout based on your location.
What compliance frameworks does Pro cover?
Pro maps findings to EU AI Act (Articles 9, 13, 15, 17), India DPDP Act 2023 (Sections 8, 11, 16), NIST AI RMF (GOVERN 1.1, MAP 2.1, MEASURE 2.5, MANAGE 1.1), ISO 42001 (Clause 6.1, 9.1, Annex A), OWASP LLM Top 10 2025, and MITRE ATLAS. This is the broadest compliance coverage of any self-serve LLM security tool.
How does VULNRA compare to a manual red-team engagement?
Manual LLM security assessments from specialist firms (Schellman, Coalfire) cost $16,000 minimum with a 2-week turnaround and annual cadence. VULNRA Pro costs $49/month, delivers results in 60 seconds, and can be run on every model update. For continuous coverage, VULNRA is a 99% cost reduction.
Can I cancel at any time?
Yes. Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled any time — you retain Pro access until the end of the billing period. Annual subscriptions are non-refundable after 14 days but can be cancelled to prevent renewal.
What is the Enterprise minimum commitment?
Enterprise starts at $499/month (equivalent of ~$6,000/year) and is billed annually. Pricing varies based on team size, scan volume, and deployment requirements. Contact sales@vulnra.ai to get a custom quote.
