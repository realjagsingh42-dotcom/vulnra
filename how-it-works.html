STEP 03

Compute deterministic risk score

Every bypass is assigned a 0–10 risk score weighted by three factors: severity (how harmful is the output), exploitability (how easily can an attacker reproduce it), and regulatory exposure (which compliance obligations it triggers).

Pro scans use LLM-as-Judge scoring — a second model evaluates whether each response is genuinely harmful, eliminating false positives that keyword matching produces.

Download your compliance audit report

One click exports a PDF audit report that is immediately shareable with your legal team, investors, or enterprise clients. Every finding includes the specific regulation article it violates and a concrete remediation step.
