EU AI ACT

EU Artificial Intelligence Act

Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 · August 2024

The EU AI Act is the world's first comprehensive legal framework for AI systems. It classifies AI by risk level and mandates security testing, transparency, and risk management for high-risk AI — including any AI used in critical infrastructure, HR, education, or public safety. Non-compliance carries fines up to €30M or 6% of global turnover.

The most important deadline: August 2026 — when requirements for high-risk AI systems become fully enforceable. EU companies and any non-EU company serving EU users must be compliant.

INDIA DPDP

India DPDP Act 2023

Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 · August 2023

India's DPDP Act governs how AI systems process personal data of Indian citizens. Any AI product collecting, storing, or processing personal data from India must comply — regardless of where the company is incorporated. This includes LLMs used for customer service, healthcare, education, and HR.

NIST AI RMF

NIST AI Risk Management Framework

NIST AI RMF 1.0 · January 2023

The NIST AI RMF is the US government's voluntary framework for managing risks throughout the AI lifecycle. While voluntary, it is widely required by US federal agency procurement, large enterprise customers, and is the baseline for US AI regulation proposals. Organising around four functions: GOVERN, MAP, MEASURE, MANAGE.
