The Origin Story

A $16,000 quote,
a laptop, and curiosity.

In late 2023, while building an AI-native healthcare application, we went looking for a way to verify its security. We knew about prompt injection and data exfiltration, but there was no tool to test for them automatically.

We called a specialized security firm. Their quote? $16,000 for a one-time audit of a single chatbot. For a bootstrapped startup in India, that wasn't just expensive — it was a barrier to innovation.

We realized that if we were facing this, every AI developer was too. Security shouldn't be a luxury for the Enterprise. It should be a fundamental utility for anyone shipping a system_prompt.

VULNRA was born to bridge that gap. We open-sourced our core mapping logic and built a premium dashboard to make professional AI red-teaming accessible to everyone.
