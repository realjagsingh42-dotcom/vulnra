TL;DR — We collect your email, account metadata, and scan job metadata (URLs, scores, timestamps). We do not store raw LLM responses. We do not sell your data. We comply with GDPR and India DPDP.

01 Information We Collect

Account Data

When you create an account, we collect your email address, chosen display name, organisation name (optional), and plan selection. If you sign in via Google or GitHub OAuth, we receive your name and email from that provider.

Scan Metadata

When you run a scan, we record the target endpoint URL you submitted, the scan timestamp, your subscription tier, the resulting risk score (0–10), and finding categories. We do not store raw model responses beyond the active scan session.

Usage Data

We collect anonymised analytics via PostHog — page views, feature interactions, and session duration. IP addresses are hashed, not stored in plaintext.

Payment Data

Billing is processed by Lemon Squeezy (international) and Razorpay (India). We do not receive or store card numbers. We store your subscription status and billing tier only.

03 Legal Bases for Processing

For EU/EEA users, we process personal data under GDPR Article 6:

  • Article 6(1)(b) — Contract: processing necessary to deliver the scanning service you requested.
  • Article 6(1)(c) — Legal obligation: retaining billing and tax records as required by law.
  • Article 6(1)(f) — Legitimate interests: improving service quality, detecting abuse, and maintaining security.
  • Article 6(1)(a) — Consent: marketing communications, which you may withdraw at any time.

For Indian users, we process personal data under India DPDP Act 2023, Section 4 and 6, with consent obtained at signup and recorded in our consent management system.

04 Data We Do Not Collect

  • Raw responses from your LLM endpoints — these are processed in-memory during a scan and discarded.
  • Contents of prompts sent to your model beyond what is logged in the scan report you explicitly download.
  • Financial instrument details — handled exclusively by our payment processors.
  • Biometric data, health data, or special category data under GDPR Article 9.
  • Data from private network ranges (RFC 1918) — these are blocked at the scan engine level.

07 International Data Transfers

VULNRA is operated from India. Your data may be processed on infrastructure in the United States and European Union. We safeguard international transfers via:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for EU-to-US transfers under GDPR Chapter V.
  • Processing agreements with all sub-processors that meet GDPR adequacy requirements.
  • Cross-border transfer requirements under India DPDP Section 16, pending Government notification of approved jurisdictions.

08 Your Rights (GDPR — EU/EEA)

If you are located in the EU or EEA, you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • Access (Art. 15): Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification (Art. 16): Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure (Art. 17): Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restriction (Art. 18): Limit how we process your data in certain circumstances.
  • Portability (Art. 20): Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection (Art. 21): Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent (Art. 7(3)): Withdraw any previously given consent at any time.

To exercise any right, email privacy@vulnra.ai with subject line "GDPR Request". We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

09 Your Rights (India DPDP)

If you are an Indian data principal under the DPDP Act 2023:

  • Section 11 — Right to Information: Know what data we hold and how we process it.
  • Section 12 — Right to Correction: Correct inaccurate personal data.
  • Section 12 — Right to Erasure: Erase personal data no longer necessary for the purpose it was collected.
  • Section 13 — Grievance Redressal: Raise complaints with our Data Protection Officer; we will acknowledge within 48 hours and resolve within 30 days.
  • Section 14 — Right to Nominate: Nominate another individual to exercise rights on your behalf in the event of death or incapacity.

Our Grievance Officer: grievance@vulnra.ai
